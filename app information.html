Ballistics Buddy consists of two setups:

  • (1) Rifle Setup

    • Input the required information about your rifle setup and cartridge/ammunition. Save your rifle setup with a unique setup name. You can save multiple different setups.

  • (2) Elevation and Windage

    • Once your setup is saved, you can calculate the Elevation and Windage adjustments. Enter the distance to the target, barrel angle, and wind velocity (if the wind is significant) and then click calculate!
